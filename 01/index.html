dreams from long ago
an open labyrinth

I dreamt that I saw the moon over the horizon

when it got closer I saw it was a brand new world

on this new planet was my childhood home

(but it was not my home)

it was a home filled with stairs

I wandered in the darkness with a candle
(I knew you were inside)

down all the stairs, in the basement, covered in water

there you were
